Self-Defense Disclaimer!
This is my page about firearms and perosnal self-defense. This content is not for the faint of heart.

This firearm is my regular CCW carry weapon. I train with it regularly to ensure compliance with licensing to use this in self-defense if necessary while in the public. The Beretta M9 9mm chambered configuration is a civilian variant of the 92fs which is also what the U.S. Military based their military variant on M9. Until recently U.S. Armed forces at large have used this side-arm.

This is my Rock Island Armory 1911. Chambered in .45 ACP the 1911 was one of the first produced single-action (initial hammer pull) semi-automatic for the U.S. consumption by John Moses Browning a mormon gunsmith. The .45 ACP is a slower round but is known for stopping power.
